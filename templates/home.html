What is Kolam?

Kolam is a traditional South Indian art form where intricate patterns are drawn using rice flour. These designs, often created by women, adorn the entrances of homes and temples, symbolizing auspiciousness and welcoming deities. Kolam patterns are based on mathematical principles, combining symmetry, geometry, and creativity.
